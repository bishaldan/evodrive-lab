\section{Limitations}

EvoDrive Lab is a simplified 2D benchmark and should not be interpreted as a realistic autonomous driving simulator. The benchmark scale is modest, the procedural suites are small, and the PPO baseline is intentionally lightweight. The paper therefore focuses on comparative generalization trends inside this benchmark, not on real-world driving relevance or state-of-the-art claims.

These limitations are important features of the paper's honesty. The value of EvoDrive Lab lies in reproducibility, visibility, and compact comparative experimentation, not in environmental realism.
