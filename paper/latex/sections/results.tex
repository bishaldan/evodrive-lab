\section{Results}

The paper reports validation and held-out test performance using completion, reward, crash rate, and mean episode length. The primary comparison focuses on whether the ranking of methods on validation tracks matches their behavior on unseen test tracks.

Figures generated by the paper plotting pipeline are intended to summarize:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
    \item validation versus test completion by algorithm,
    \item test reward by algorithm,
    \item test crash rate by algorithm,
    \item completion under sensor-count and track-length ablations.
\end{itemize}

The final manuscript should also include a small qualitative replay table that highlights one strong run, one median run, and one clear failure case per algorithm.
